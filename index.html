René Armenta

blending art
with improvisation
and spiritual intention

Exploring how Art can summon ideas and wisdom from within. As we experience art, we self-generate ideas, stories, and meaningfulness. I believe if we practice tracking, documenting and becoming aware of these images and narratives as we enjoy art, we can begin to witness ourselves in new ways and begin to harness new insights from within. Music and abstract visuals allow us to self-generate context and meaningfulness. We are simply not taught to pay attention and develop that awareness. There is an untapped wealth of inner wisdom if we take the time to practice playing, listening, and looking deeply into abstract, geometric, and sacred imagery. My work seeks to create a backdrop ... a foundation ... a field of flow and depth to explore streams of imagery and ideas. As a musician I explore many genres from orchestral to electronic to soundtrack to art rock to meditation to sound bath to ethnic/world and shamanic journey work. As a visual artist I have always loved abstract painting, sacred geometry, labyrinths, cave paintings, ancient civilizations, illuminated texts, glyphs, mystical imagery, maps, schematics, cryptic writing, calligraphy, the renaissance, science fiction and magickal diagrams. My paintings tend to blend many of these qualities into my work. For over 20 years I have been exploring the art of improvisation in my music, painting and live performance. This is the juice ... the zone ... the open channel to create and be completely in the moment and surrender to the creative flow into ARTiculation.

bio

composer • visualizer • animator

Based in the Denver/Boulder area, René Armenta is a dynamic composer and motion graphics artist specializing in evocative music and visualizations for distinctive narratives. René's intuitive, improvisational scores, featured in films like A Thousand Pieces (2020), Dogoda (2024), and Trance: The Cathy O'Brien Story (2022), create immersive sonic experiences. René has also released several instrumental albums, blending ambient and journey exploration music to inspire listeners to craft their own inner narratives, with these works and select scores accessible on leading streaming platforms.

From 2010 to 2016, René Armenta designed visual presentations for the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences' Oscar Nominations, honing technical expertise that he now channels into visualizing trending topics such as UFO experiences, Disclosure, and Secret Space Program narratives. As a solo artist wielding just a MacBook Pro, René crafts stunning 3D visualizations and animations, showcased in his demo reel, bringing complex ideas to life with remarkable ingenuity. This fusion of mainstream precision and visionary storytelling distinguishes René's work, catering to audiences and filmmakers exploring unconventional themes. With seven film credits and a growing portfolio, René is dedicated to creating music and visuals that resonate deeply with audiences exploring non-traditional narratives, offering innovative artistry for a new era of storytelling.

Original Music

Now available on most streaming services

Dreamscapes

A 30-minute, 8-track ambient electronic journey of layered synthesizer improvisations. Modal harmonies, pedal tones, and cluster tensions weave a cosmic labyrinth. No beats, no hooks—just evocative textures for dreamers and seekers.

TRANCE: The Cathy O'Brien Story

An 18-track suite in three movements to reflect the film's journey through (i) Mind Control, (ii) Human Slavery, and (iii) Recovery. Improvised layers of Acoustic and Electronic textures blend together for a deep musical journey.

A Thousand Pieces

A contemplative score for orchestra composed during the turbulent summer of 2020. Featuring a solo trumpet and military snare as recurring sonic characters that reflect upon the awakening of a people within a country in turmoil.

Into The Deep

A 12-track suite of improvised piano played over synthesized drone textures. This 90-minute session was recorded live during an actual Restorative Yoga session. The musical performance reflects the restorative journey of the class as it unfolded.

Stonywood

Recorded during an intimate outdoor concert in 2010, this 10-track suite provides over an hour of solo improvised piano. Contemplative and moody, these tracks explore light and dark tones and were performed as the sun set on Summer Solstice.

Invocations

My first recorded suite of pure live improvisation - there are no edits. Recorded directly from a Kawaii ES1, this live performance provided the musical backdrop for a gallery show; these are the best performances from that evening. Perfect for non-intrusive contemplation.

Oracle Decks

tools to explore your own inner-wisdom

FIOSO

FIOSO is a 45 card deck composed of abstract line drawings with the intention to bring the "reader" into an intuitive state; to bring insight and meaning to a query without an "authority" dictating that meaning. Rather, the "reader" becomes the "lens" that brings focus and meaning into each reading.

The intention is to pierce through the trappings of the “trained mind” and activate into the “cosmic mind.” The “trained mind” is temporary, earth based, linear, programmable, centered around the ego and focused on what it can touch, taste, see, hear, smell and quantify. The “cosmic mind” is the aspect of awareness which is tapped into the infinite, the invisible beyond the 5-senses. It is limitless, non-linear, morphic & mysterious.

By avoiding the use of language (or pre-determined meaning), the "reader" is then facilitated to bring their OWN interpretation into language and meaning for themselves. As such, FIOSO automatically promotes the process of self-authority and self-discernment.

PATHWAYS

PATHWAYS is a unique 34 card hexagon shaped oracle deck that opens your mind to your inner wisdom through interactive play.

With PATHWAYS, you create mandala like maps/grids of connectivity to stimulate your intuitive self. Each card can inspire a phrase or sentence to describe what you see. With multiple cards you create your own narrative revealing your true inner wisdom and path.

With PATHWAYS, the strategy is to DISCOVER. There is nothing to learn, no system to memorize, no instructions to follow, no right or wrong way to use the cards. You become the "lens" that brings focus and meaning to the cards. 

Through the dynamics of game-play, PATHWAYS is a tool to excavate your DEEP self. It creates an interactive schematic you can use to examine your soul’s trajectory and witness the causal forces that may be at work beyond time and space.
